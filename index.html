SF. The Bay Area. Silicon Valley. Home to the largest tech hub in the world. Honerable mention though include the Giants, 49'ers and Too Short. San Francisco has many dining options you can add to your passport.

New York City

The Big Apple. The Concrete Jungle. The City That Never Sleeps. Whether you're craving take-out or wanting eat at the restaurant Beyonce was at lastnight, New York City has it all. Diversify your passport with some of the most popular spots in NYC.

London

The Queen of England calls this place home. The most populous city in the United Kingdom. The capital of England. London is filled with top eateries, bakeries and tea shops. People all over the world call London their home and have brought their culture & cuisuine to share. Try some Jamaican or Nigerian food while you're near the Big Ben and add it to your passport.
